    Bio

    Greetings!

    My name is Billy. You can call me Bill it won't bother me.

    I am from the indianapolis area, specifically the southwest side. I have an amazing wife and 2 beautiful girls.

    In the past, I was a material handler/dock worker. When that company decided to shut it's location down during the pandemic I knew I had to strive for something more. This was a difficult time to get through. I knew I needed to make a change. I needed to find a career path that I would ultimately find joy in. That is what brought me to Elevenfifty Academy.

    I found a desire that I had been missing in my job. I became a Software Developer. I found joy, a desire to create, a never ending path of learning. I finally found that missing piece. I enjoy the troubleshooting, the creativity it takes to find a solution to a problem.

    I have always been a hard worker determined to get the job done and get it done right. After Elevenfifty, I now have a sense of purpose that I lacked before. I have the confidence in my abilities and the need to elevate my skills. This field also allows me to surround myself with people who will challenge how I think, broadening my perspective.

    I have recently joined the Pathfinder program with Salesforce. I am excited for the abundance of possibilities, that one choice, one decision has opened up.
